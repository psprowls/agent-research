<objective>
Two lint defects on a freshly-bootstrapped + scanned wiki: (1) emitted wikilinks
in package overview / context / domain overview templates lack the workspace-rooted
`wiki/` prefix, so 40 links resolve as broken against `link_targets` (which are
keyed workspace-relative); (2) `wiki/index.md` always emits `## More` links to
five section indexes (`concepts`, `sources`, `adrs`, `architecture`,
`dependencies`) but `init_vault.py` only creates `dependencies/index.md`,
producing 4 broken links on every fresh wiki.

Fix: patch the three offending templates to emit `[[wiki/<container>/...]]`
forms; create stub `index.md` files in the four missing section dirs during
`init_wiki`. Re-run scan + lint against the real wiki and confirm zero broken
links from these two categories.
</objective>
