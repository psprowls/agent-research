<objective>
Stamp `schema_version: 1` on every JSONL record produced by the three trace writers in the project (the two `SubagentPool` writers and the `query.py` `query_summary` writer), and lock the field's presence with focused unit tests. This is the producer half of OBS-04 — schema versioning. The renderer (consumer half) is addressed in plan 09-05.

Purpose: Per locked decisions D-01 (`schema_version` on every record, not as a file header) and D-02 (integer format, `schema_version: 1`), every line in `.code-wiki/traces/*.jsonl` must be self-describing going forward. This is a purely additive change protected by the Phase 8 D-06/D-07 additive-shape rule.

Output: Three writers each carry one extra key. Existing tests still pass; new tests prove the field is always written for success, error, and batch-cancellation paths.
</objective>
