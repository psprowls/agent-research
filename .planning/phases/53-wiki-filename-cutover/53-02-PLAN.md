<objective>
Delete `encode_slug` and `decode_slug` from `wiki_io.entity_writer`; rewrite every consumer
call site in `packages/` and `agents/` to read `frontmatter.uri` directly or to call
`short_filename(uri, collision_set, ...)` from Phase 52; close the test-fixture gap in
`tests/fixtures/round-trip-vault/`; delete any test that exercised the removed functions.

left the functions in place (Phase 52 D-09 deferral). Phase 53 closes that deferral and
removes the dead surface. Per CONTEXT D-04..D-07.

- `packages/wiki-io/src/wiki_io/entity_writer.py` with `encode_slug`, `decode_slug` deleted; `_ADMITTED_URI_PREFIXES` deleted if orphaned.
- `packages/wiki-io/src/wiki_io/link_rewriter.py` rewritten to read `frontmatter.uri` directly.
- `packages/wiki-io/src/wiki_io/index_generator.py` rewritten to call `short_filename`.
- Any scanner-side `decode_slug` call site under `agents/graph-wiki-agent/` rewritten to read `frontmatter.uri`.
- `packages/wiki-io/tests/test_entity_writer.py` (or wherever the legacy tests live) updated: tests of `encode_slug`/`decode_slug` deleted; tests of the rewritten call sites verified or added.
- `packages/wiki-io/tests/fixtures/round-trip-vault/` confirmed/updated to short-form scheme.
