<objective>
Fill the 7 existing `entity-<kind>.md` templates with the meaningful prose migrated from the
legacy per-kind `overview.md` (and `app/testing.md`) pages, and convert each template's
data-bearing body H1 from an angle placeholder (`# <Package Name>`) to a double-brace token
(`# {{package_name}}`) so the scanner (Plan 01) can substitute the real name. Human/LLM-authored
sections become visible `> TODO: <instructions>` blockquotes. Instruction-style `<...>`
placeholders inside guidance are LEFT as angles (D-01). This is ENTITY-01 (migration) + ENTITY-02
(ontology sections + TODO convention).

Purpose: today the entity templates are thin scaffolds whose bodies are emitted verbatim, so
generated pages show `# <Package Name>` and lack the curated per-kind sections the legacy
overview pages had. After this plan the templates carry real ontology structure, and their
data-bearing headings are `{{...}}` tokens ready for Plan 01's substitution.

Output: 7 filled `entity-<kind>.md` templates honoring the two-token rule (D-01), the curated
migration map (D-08/D-09), and the TODO blockquote convention (D-11/D-12); plus extended
`test_entity_templates.py` assertions.
</objective>
