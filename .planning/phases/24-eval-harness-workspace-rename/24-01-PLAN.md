internal `_vault_content_hash` renames to `_wiki_content_hash(wiki)` AND its baseline JSON

a CHECK 5 block banning re-introduction of `vault_path:` function params, bare `vault: Path`,
and `"--vault"` argparse literals scoped to `packages/eval-harness/{src,tests}`.

window where the src side is renamed but 194 test refs are still red.
